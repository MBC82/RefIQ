📚 Law 12 — GK Restrictions (2025/26)

Under the 2025/26 IFAB Laws, a goalkeeper who holds the ball longer than permitted is penalized with a corner kick for the opposing team — not an indirect free kick. The opponent's verbal protest has no effect on the restart; any dissent should be dealt with separately.
